To Get Updates About Library and Its Blogs

The Library frequently adds new links to free, online articles and new posts in its numerous blogs.

You can easily get notified of updates. Here's how:

Do You Want to Automatically Be Notified of New Blog Posts?

1. Just "Follow" the Library's Twitter account -- and that's very easy and quick to do.

2. You need your own Twitter account (it takes about 3 minutes to create an account at
http://www.twitter.com. You'll end up on your own Twitter account page).

3. Go to the Library's Twitter account (or click on this icon ) and click on "Follow".

Each new post will result in a message on your Twitter page. The message will have the name of the post and its Web address.
