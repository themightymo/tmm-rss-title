Some Key Books Focused on the Field of Organization Development (OD)

Assembled by Carter McNamara, MBA, PhD

Note that the field of OD encompasses a wide variety of business-, management- and organization-related practices. Therefore, a list of OD-related books could well be exhaustive in length. The following are some of the major books focused primarily on the field of OD. This short list is by no means meant to represent the singular "foundation" texts in OD. Rather, the list is a good starting point from which to gain some basic understanding of the profession of OD.

Some Major Texts About the Field of OD

Organization Development and Change
Sixth Edition, by Cummings and Worley. South-Western Publishing, 1997. Available from Amazon Books.

Practicing Organization Development : A Guide for Consultants
by Rothwell (Editor), Roland Sullivan (Editor), Gary N. McLean. Pfeiffer & Co., 1995. Available from Amazon Books.

Organization Development Classics : The Practice and Theory of Change -- The Best of the OD Practitioner
by Van Eynde (Editor), Judith C. Hoy (Editor), Dixie cod Van Eynde, Dixie Cody Van Eynde (Editor). Jossey-Bass, 1997. Available from Amazon Books.
