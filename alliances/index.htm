Strategic Alliances: Collaborations, Mergers and Acquisitions, Joint Ventures

One of the most powerful means to strengthen an organization's impact and sustainability is by engaging in a strategic alliance or combining with other organizations. There are a variety of ways this can be structured. Nonprofits face pressure from funders to collaborate with other nonprofits. Mergers and acquisitions are a very prominent strategy, especially among large businesses. This topic explains collaborations, mergers and acquisitions, and joint ventures.
