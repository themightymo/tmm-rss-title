Workplace Bullying

Workplace bullying is a form of employee harassment of one or more persons, by one or more perpetrators, that takes one or more of the following forms: verbal abuse, offensive conduct/behaviors (including nonverbal) which are threatening, humiliating or intimidating, and work interference (sabotage) which prevents work from getting done.
