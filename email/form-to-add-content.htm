To Add a Link to the Library

LINKS WE DO ACCEPT:

We're glad to consider your submission if it is directly to a FREE article or a web page that contains substantive, practical information about personal, professional or organizational development.

LINKS WE WILL NOT ACCEPT:

1. We won't accept a link -- to your home page where users have to look around to find links to your free resources.

2. We won't accept a link -- to a site where users must register or login.

3. We won't accept a link -- to a resource that users must pay to use.

4. We won't accept a link -- to a "free trial" to use a resource for a short period and then pay for it.

5. We prefer links to articles that are at least 500 words in length.
